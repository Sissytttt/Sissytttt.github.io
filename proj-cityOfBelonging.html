City Of Belonging

“City of Belonging” is an augmented reality-powered mobile application, revolutionizing the way individuals from diverse cultural backgrounds navigate new territories. Designed to empower and connect, this app embarks users on enriching journeys, fostering a profound sense of belonging while seamlessly bridging cultures and nurturing a supportive global community for those embarking on new country experiences.

Key Words:
UX Research, UI Design, Augmented Reality

Oct 2022 - Apr 2023

Technology:
Lens Studio
Adobe
Sketch

Individual Project

Being an expatriate over the past years, I've connected emotionally with people navigating life away from home. "City of Belonging" is featured with map-based memory storage, personalized navigation, social interaction, and cultural exploration recording, enhancing users' interactions with the city. The highlight is an interactive augmented reality experience, bridging diverse cultures through historical relics, mythology, and nostalgia, fostering spiritual connections between the user and the place. Embarking users on enriching journeys, the app fosters a profound sense of belonging while seamlessly bridging cultures and nurturing a supportive global community for those embarking on new country experiences. Throughout this process, informed by user research and psychology studies, I've realized that design entails a nuanced understanding and connection of human nature, emotions, and culture, seamlessly integrated through technology and aesthetics. This transformative journey has also enabled me to integrate different cultures within my sense of cultural identity.
