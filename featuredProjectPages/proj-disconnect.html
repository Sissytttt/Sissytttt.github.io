Disconnect

"Disconnect" is a large-scale installation crafted from over 300 cassettes and enhanced with sensors, projections, and lighting. This conceptual piece contends that instant, less thoughtful interactions in the digital age detrimentally impact personal relationships, fostering isolation and ineffective emotion conveyance.

Key Words:
Interactive Installation, Digital Communication

Jun 2022 - Sep 2022

Technology:
Arduino
Fabrication
Projections

Individual Project

In a world dominated by digital communication, Disconnect emerges as a large-scale interactive installation, a poignant reflection on the diminishing sincerity in contemporary human connections. By interacting with symbolic elements above the cloud, the audience is immersed in a visually impactful experience, prompting contemplation on the evolving nature of their digital-age relationships. This artistic endeavor sparks meaningful dialogue about the authenticity of modern communication through its immersive space and interactive design.
