In a world where friendships span continents, The Challenge of Long-Distance Connection lies in capturing the warmth of shared moments—like clinking glasses or sharing a toast together.

For centuries, The Culture Of Drinking has been a universal ritual for connection, symbolizing hospitality, friendship, and togetherness. A drink carries more than flavor; it carries emotions and memories.

MIXI brings the physicality of shared drinking into your long-distance connections, offering a multi-sensory experience that combines voice, visual, taste, and emotion. Each personalized drink goes beyond sharing a drink—it's a bridge that transforms emotional conversations into a meaningful, tangible experience.

MIXI is a beverage machine designed to transform long-distance social connection into interactive and multi-sensory experiences. Building on the universal tradition of connecting over food and drinks, MIXI uses AI to analyze users' spoken conversations, detecting the emotions embedded within and visualizing them on circumplex model of emotion. By gathering and interpreting this emotional data, MIXI generates a personalized drink recipe that reflects the user’s overall emotional state. The machine then crafts the drink, allowing participants to share and experience each other’s emotions through taste. MIXI bridges physical distance by offering a unique way to connect, fostering empathy and deepening bonds through shared emotional expressions.
