True Dream Home

The True Dream Home experience, a VR collaboration with House Flip by Fun-Gi Game, targets middle-aged women, the game's major audience. It offers them the opportunity to create and immerse themselves in their dream homes, complete with personalized interior designs and dream locations, through the innovative use of virtual reality technology.

The True Dream Home experience leverages VR technology to allow middle-aged women to design and immerse themselves in their dream homes. Through detailed user research, we identified key user interests, preferences, and pain points, including struggles with technology, leading to a tailored and tutorial-based VR experience that combines realistic environments with interactive design elements. Our competitive analysis informed development, and usability testing iteratively improved the interface and functionality. The final product offers instructional tutorials, interactive and customizable home environments, reduced motion sickness, and future plans to expand features.

Problem Statement

The one True Dream Home experience gives middle-aged women (25-53 years old) an opportunity to create and immerse in their dream home with their desired interior design and dream location by introducing the use of virtual reality technology

UX Research annd Analysis

User Persona

Interview Finding

- Most interviewees are familiar with mobile apps and social media, but not with XR technology.

- Dream homes typically feature nature-adjacent locations, open spaces, and customization options for furniture and design.

- High interest in the True Dream Home experience, especially in decorating and switching between different living environments.

- Concerns include the difficulty and sickness of handling VR technology and the need for onboarding instructions.
