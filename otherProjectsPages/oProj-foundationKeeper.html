An app-based makeup device for visually impaired individuals

Our goal is to create an inclusive foundation device for visually impaired individuals, utilizing face recognition, UV detection, and AI voice guidance. By ensuring even application and providing step-by-step assistance, our innovative product not only enhances the makeup experience for the visually impaired but also offers a relaxed and accessible solution for all users.

Big Idea

To achieve our goal of producing an innovative foundation make-up for visually impaired people, we came up with our applicable idea, which focuses on a device that can assist them in applying foundation evenly across the face. Everyone has the right to pursue beauty. The foundation make-up is the most basic step for people to cover the flaws on their faces and become more confident. At the same time, applying the foundation evenly on the face is not easy for all makeup lovers, let alone for those with visual impairment. From our perspective, designing a foundation product that provides easier access for people with visual impairment to makeup is the first step and essential step to promoting an inclusive makeup trend. On a broader scale, our innovative foundation can provide help to all consumers who are struggling with applying the foundation. We’ll be able to guarantee a more relaxing makeup process for all individuals interested in applying makeup.

Our goal is to produce an inclusive foundation device with applicable technologies for visually impaired consumers. Our devices can use face recognition to detect the user’s face, use a UV detection device to check the foundation’s evenness, and use an AI voice guide to send out voice commands to guide the user to better apply the foundation. It contains three main functions, including the UV detector, AI voice guide, and face recognition technology. After research, we found that there are existing products containing relevant technologies. First, Sunsceenr can image the UV and detect sunscreen application. With this technology, our device can detect the UV absorbed by the user’s foundation makeup and guide the application. Then, face recognition technology also has been well developed and pervasively utilized. For instance, the iPhone’s face recognition system guarantees users to unlock their phones without inputting passwords. As for AI voice guides, current products like Tmall Genie and Xiaomi Box have achieved the function of communicating with the user and reporting information. These technologies above ensure that our product is applicable and doable. We hope that our product can further bring the inclusive brand image of L’oréal to everyone.
