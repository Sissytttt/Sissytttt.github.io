Real Me

Chow Tai Fook Branding Competition
Shanghai Champion

The project focuses on creating a unique jewelry brand that celebrates individuality and self-expression. The concept is embodied through a series of specially designed jewelry products and a pop-up store experience that is featured with six distinctive themes and interactive elements. The pop-up store design is aimed at engaging users and fostering strong emotional connections between customers and the brand. We hope this design not only showcases the brand's philosophy but also enhances customer experience and engagement.
