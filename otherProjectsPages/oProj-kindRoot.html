Kind Root

2 players competitive platformer game
Global Game Jam 2023

KindRoot is a competitive platformer game for 2 players – each player is tied up to a root on the ground and in order to reach the destination on top, they need to grow their roots into a tree by collecting sunshine, jumping around, and competing with the other player. The first player to arrive at the top is the winner of the game. This year’s theme of ‘roots’ is embedded in the core mechanics of our game – In addition to the gameplay, our character design and scene setting also fit into the narrative of ‘roots’, such as seeds, sunshine and soil. For our prop design, we want to create an entertaining experience by encouraging the players to interact with each other – for example, the knife item allows players to cut off each other’s vine and fall off the platforms by jumping across those vines. BTW, we are a team that has 13 hours time difference!
