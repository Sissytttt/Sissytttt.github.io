BeautifyAI

An AI-powered makeup app design
2023 L'oreal Brainstorm

BeautifyAI is an innovative AI-driven makeup application that leverages Augmented Reality and Web 3.0 trends to enhance user experiences. The app is designed to operate as a social community platform where users receive AI-generated makeup suggestions based on facial recognition, personal preferences, and a makeup toolkit. Additionally, the app provides real-time augmented reality tutorials to guide users through makeup application. The project also includes the development of commercialization strategies and plans to expand the app's functionality to incorporate open world features in the future.
