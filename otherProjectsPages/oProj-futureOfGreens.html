Future Of Greens

A Blend of Virtual Immersive Animation And Physical Sculpture

"Future of Greens" delves into the anthropocene's impact on nature. Exploring the repercussions of rapid urbanization, industrialization, and climate change, the project envisions a future where artificial alternatives replace dwindling natural environments. The multimedia installation comprises a virtual environment, blending nature with mechanics, and a physical installation replicating a traditional natural setting using industrial materials.
