Whispering Sanctuary

Urban public installation for citizen's emotional dwelling.

Whispering Sanctuary is an installation that blends urban living with the therapeutic embrace of nature. The Tree Hole Installations is trying to offer the citizens a sacred space to unburden their emotions and transform these expressions into the soothing sounds of nature. Users can hug the tree, and share their thoughts, worries, and secrets into the integrated microphone. As their voices echo through the installation, it is played back to the city converting it to the sound of nature, creating a harmonious exchange between the urban dweller and the natural world. Whispering Sanctuary not only serves as a refuge for emotional release but also fosters a profound connection between individuals and the restorative power of nature.
